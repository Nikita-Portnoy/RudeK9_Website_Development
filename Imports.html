Imports

Only German Shepherd Dogs bred from proven working lines have the true commitment and drives to overcome various tasks and pressures that lie within these tasks. Temperament, character, nature, courage and full commitment is what we ask of our dogs and remember you will only get this from the true German Shepherd Dog who is bred from 100% working lines.
