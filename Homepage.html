    Training

    Too many dogs are given up after their normal, easily modifiable behaviors like barking, biting, chewing, and house soiling are allowed to become problems. But it doesn't have to be this way. To prevent your dog from becoming a sad statistic, take your dog and your family to a professional dog training class. Our training classes are a fun and social activity that helps your dog become a well-behaved, safe, and valued family member.

    Imports

    We specialize only in the very best of Working Bloodlines suitable for schutzhund and working trials We only import from the very finest 100% working lines. Only German Shepherd Dogs bred from proven working lines have the true commitment and drives to overcome various tasks and pressures that lie within these tasks.     Puppies

    We breed only the top quality working German Shepherd bloodlines imported directly from Europe. Harvey Allen has been breeding German Shepherds for over 32 years and is very knowledgeable in temperment testing to ensure that we provide only the best possible puppy for you and your family. All of our dogs have been tested and are clear of DM, Hips and spinal problems. Our puppies come complete with shots and deworming and are cared for by local veterinarians to keep our puppies generally healthy. Suitable for Schutzhund, police work and being a loyal family member. We start early socialization with children and adults alike.
